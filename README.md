# Ice Fashing Wind Calculator - App Store Content

## Subtitle
Predict fish feeding zones based on wind patterns

## Description
Ice Fashing Wind Calculator is the ultimate tool for ice anglers who want to find the most productive fishing spots based on wind conditions. Using proven fishing principles, this app analyzes wind direction and strength to predict where fish will be feeding most actively on your lake or pond.

**Key Features:**
• Interactive wind-based feeding zone predictions
• Visual map showing hot zones, active zones, and slow zones
• Multiple water body shapes (round, oval, irregular)
• Instant calculations based on current wind conditions
• Save and review calculation history
• Compare different wind scenarios
• Detailed explanations of why wind affects fish behavior
• Works completely offline - perfect for remote locations

**The Wind-Fish Connection:**
Wind creates currents, oxygenates water, and pushes food sources to specific areas. Fish concentrate in these zones to feed efficiently, especially under the ice where movement is limited.

**Perfect for:**
- Finding productive spots on large lakes
- Understanding how wind affects fish positioning
- Saving time by fishing the right areas
- Adapting to changing wind conditions throughout the day

**What You Get:**
Simple wind input, instant zone prediction, and a visual map showing exactly where to drill your holes for maximum success.

Stop wandering on the ice. Ice Fashing Wind Calculator points you to where the fish are feeding right now.
